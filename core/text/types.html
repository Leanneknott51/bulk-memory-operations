Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef3137#1{{}}\mathdef3137{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

In future versions of WebAssembly, this scheme may be extended to support multiple results or more general result types.

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef3137#1{{}}\mathdef3137{parameter} & \def\mathdef3207#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3207{(}~~\def\mathdef3208#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3208{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3209#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3209{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3210#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3210{(}~~\def\mathdef3211#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3211{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3212#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3212{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef3137#1{{}}\mathdef3137{result} & \def\mathdef3213#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3213{(}~~\def\mathdef3214#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3214{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3215#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3215{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3216#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3216{(}~~\def\mathdef3217#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3217{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3218#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3218{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
